PACER Methodology

  • Prospect - Identify and qualify potential clients
  • Assess - Understand client needs and pain points
  • Challenge - Present solutions that address client challenges
  • Execute - Implement the solution and deliver value
  • Retain - Maintain and grow the client relationship
